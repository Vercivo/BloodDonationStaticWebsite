About Us

Connecting generous donors with patients in need-quickly, safely, and with dignity.

Our Mission

Every drop counts. We operate a community-powered network that mobilizes donors, supports hospitals and clinics, and raises awareness for the constant need for safe blood. By simplifying how people find and schedule donations, we help save lives every day.

  • Build and support a reliable donor community.
  • Improve access to timely, safe blood transfusions.
  • Educate the public on donation eligibility and safety.
Our Promise

We keep donors informed, recipients supported, and data private. Our team partners with verified hospitals and licensed blood banks to ensure every donation meets safety standards.

Together, we're building a culture of safe, regular donation.

Eligibility Basics
  • Age 18–65, in good health.
  • Minimum weight typically 50 kg.
  • No active infections or recent major surgery.
  • Bring a valid ID on donation day.

Eligibility can vary—final assessment is made by medical staff.

Safety & Screening

All donations are carried out by trained professionals following strict hygiene and screening protocols. Each unit is tested to ensure safety for recipients.

If you're unsure about eligibility, consult a clinician at the donation center—they're here to help.
