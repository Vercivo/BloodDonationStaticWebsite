Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 September 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1) Scope

This policy applies to our website, donor registration forms, event registrations, and communications (including email and WhatsApp) operated by the Blood Donation Organization.

When you use WhatsApp to contact us, WhatsApp may process your data according to their own policies.

2) Data We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number, city.
  • Donor information: age, gender, blood group, last donation date.
  • Usage data: pages visited, interactions, approximate location (via IP).
  • Communications: messages you send via forms, email, or WhatsApp.
  • Device data: browser, device type, and settings.

3) How We Use Data

  • Register donors, coordinate donation schedules, and manage events.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Send important updates about eligibility, appointments, and campaigns.
  • Improve our website and services through analytics and feedback.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against misuse.

4) Legal Bases

  • Consent for communications and optional data (you can withdraw at any time).
  • Contract to provide requested services (e.g., registration and scheduling).
  • Legal obligation when required by law or health authority guidance.
  • Legitimate interests such as ensuring service security and awareness.

5) Sharing & Disclosure

We share data only when necessary:

  • With verified hospitals/blood banks to coordinate your donation.
  • With service providers who help us operate our site and communications.
  • With authorities if required by law or to protect safety.

We do not sell your personal data.

6) Cookies

We may use essential cookies to operate the site and optional analytics cookies to understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7) Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

8) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9) Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

10) Children

Our services are intended for adults eligible to donate blood. If you believe a minor has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11) International Transfers

If we transfer personal data across borders, we use appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable laws.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date shows the most recent version.
